Our Story

Who We Are

Bridging the Gap Between Power and Connectivity

Founded in 2010, HelioLink Solutions began with a singular vision: to create robust, self-sustaining telecommunications networks powered entirely by renewable energy. We recognized early that the future of global connectivity relies on resilient, off-grid power capabilities.

Today, we are a leading electrical engineering firm specializing in complex integrations. From commercial solar arrays atop high-rise data centers to remote mobile towers powered by hybrid microgrids, our multidisciplinary team of engineers, project managers, and technicians ensures unparalleled reliability.

Our Mission

To accelerate the global transition to sustainable energy and universal connectivity by engineering intelligent, resilient, and highly efficient infrastructure solutions that empower communities and businesses alike.

Our Vision

A fully connected world powered completely by clean, renewable energy, where geographical challenges no longer limit human potential and environmental impact is fundamentally minimized.

Leadership

Meet Our CEO

Manuel Borge Andersen

Chief Executive Officer & Founder

Manuel Borge Andersen brings over 30 years of experience in electrical engineering and power systems integration across the United Kingdom, Australia and the United States. Born in August 1967, he spent the early years of his career leading major infrastructure projects in the telecommunications and energy sectors, working across several continents.

He holds a Master's degree in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from the University of Glasgow, United Kingdom and has been instrumental in bridging the gap between renewable energy systems and telecommunications infrastructure. Manuel recognized the critical opportunity to power global communication networks through sustainable, off-grid renewable energy solutions—an insight that led to the founding of HelioLink Solutions in 2010.

Under his leadership, HelioLink has grown from a specialized consulting firm into a full-scale infrastructure deployment powerhouse with operations spanning the United Kingdom, Australia and North America, recognized twice by industry boards for excellence in sustainable engineering.
